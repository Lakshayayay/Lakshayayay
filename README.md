## 🧑‍💻 About Me

Hello! I'm **Lakshay Kalra**, a **Full-Stack Developer and AI & Automation Engineer** currently in my **3rd year of B.Tech in Computer Science** at Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies (VIPS), Delhi. My passion for technology goes beyond writing code — I architect systems, automate workflows, and build products that solve real problems.

My **core stack spans the full MERN spectrum** — from React and Next.js on the frontend to Node.js, Express, and Python on the backend — paired with hands-on experience integrating **AI/ML APIs, LLMs, and automation pipelines**. I approach engineering with the mindset of a **project manager**: thinking in systems, leading with clarity, and delivering with precision.

I actively **build and ship side projects** — from **Mentor-AI**, an AI-powered mentorship platform, to **AR/VR experiences** and **election transparency tools** — each driven by a belief that technology should create measurable, real-world impact. Beyond the keyboard, I've led teams, managed cross-functional initiatives, and competed in **hackathons**, sharpening my ability to move fast without breaking things.

I thrive on **problem-solving, rapid prototyping, and continuous learning**, always pushing the boundary between what's built and what's possible.

**Build. Automate. Scale.** 🚀

### Education 🎓
